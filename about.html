01 : About me

About Me

I have been traveling the world since 1998 acquiring a wide variety of social and professional experiences due to the different opportunities that I had the privilege to be presented with. From sophisticated hospitality establishments (Inverlochy Castle and The Caledonian/Hilton); Military Engineering; to Architectural Design and Engineering Technical Draughstmanship, sharing in common the rich diversity and the learning possibilities that each one had to offer.
I nurture art and its ideological influence through language, forms and the use of graphical representation to understand everyday objects in order to grasp a better understanding of the world around us.
I am a fast learn, curious and self taught on multiple areas, from music to drawing, 3D Modelling and digital programming. Skills that when combined with others such as the ones required in the military and semi-professional football they compile a set of tools that gives me the confidence needed to look forward to the next chapter; working for an opportunity to develop a career in web design.

Experience

Arquitemas 2013-2014; Architecture Intern. Objectives: Developing a new commercial building for the city of Guayana, Venezuela. A very rewarding experience that allowed me a constant exchange of ideas across other fields. Architectural Design, Graphisoft and Adobe Suite Platforms.
Bravaplan 2000-2002; Draughstman. Objectives: Responsible for the Engineering graphical department. CAD drafting and calculations of structural projects using predominatly autodesk platforms.

Education

FAUP Porto School of Architecture. Porto University.

Arts and Humanities based course. History and Theory of Architecture stand as the major tool for design methodology with the support of technical tools.
